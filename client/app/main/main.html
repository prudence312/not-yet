Learn Better

The hypothesis is simple: we learn better when we're presented problems that build on prior knowledge and which get incrementally harder. The slope of the difficulty from problem to problem is key! Through AI search and machine learning, our research is to find the best learning slope for every learner individually.

Built by Students

This web app was built and designed by students in support of this research. Led by industry expert Dr. Daniel Pittman, students built the tools here that allow us to create courses, keep track of students and teachers, store data related to their work, and then access that data for analysis. In addition, their services communicated with our problem generator backend in order to provide a stream of problems for students.
